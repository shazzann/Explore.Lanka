🌴 ExploreLanka
Treasure Hunt meets Travel!

ExploreLanka is a travel-based web application that turns exploration into an exciting treasure hunt experience. Whether you're a solo traveler or a thrill-seeking adventurer, ExploreLanka rewards your journey with points, a leaderboard, and a social feed—all while helping you plan your trip and get real-time travel assistance with Gemini AI.

🚀 Features
🗺️ Treasure Hunt Travel – Unlock real-world locations and earn points by visiting them.

🏆 Leaderboard – Compete with friends and fellow explorers based on your travel activity.

🤖 Gemini Travel Assistant – Get AI-powered travel help, suggestions, and guidance.

📅 Travel Planner – Create and manage your trip itinerary seamlessly.

📸 Post Feed – Share your travel experiences and discover others’ journeys.
